Balloon Pop Game

Use your index finger to pop the balloons as they rise up the screen. This exercise helps improve precision, targeting, and finger dexterity.

⏱️ Time Left: 60s

How to Play:

1. Allow camera access when prompted to enable hand tracking

2. Raise your hand in front of the camera with your index finger extended

3. Move your index finger to touch the balloons and pop them

4. Try to pop as many balloons as possible before the timer runs out

Note: Make sure you're in a well-lit area for the best hand tracking experience.
